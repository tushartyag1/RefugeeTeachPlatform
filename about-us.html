About Us

We are often charitable only once or twice a year, but refugees constrained by law are solely dependent on such funds at present. What they need is a more consistent, respectable, and stable stream of income.


Over 1.5 billion people are learning a foreign language right now as we speak. In fact, millions of them pair up with native speakers to learn these languages, and this is stastically proven!


Here at RefuTeach, we just bring you together.


RefuTeach provides a platform for refugees to teach their native languages, creating sustainable income and helping others learn new languages from native speakers.


Connecting cultures, languages, but most importantly people all across the globe.
